# Client Onboarding

This checklist defines the information Scalvia needs before cloning a WhatsApp AI assistant for a new client.

## Business Information

- Business name.
- Industry.
- City and country.
- Location or service area.
- Existing WhatsApp situation.
- Whether they have a dedicated number.
- Whether they need a new number.

## Services

- Services offered.
- Prices.
- Service duration.
- Staff or provider list.
- Required preparation instructions.
- Promotions, only if approved by the client.

## Booking

- Opening hours.
- Calendar rules.
- Booking policies.
- Cancellation policy.
- Rescheduling policy.
- Minimum notice.
- Maximum advance booking window.

## Customer Support

- FAQs.
- Escalation contact.
- Sensitive topics that require human intervention.
- Tone preferences.
- Languages.

## Systems

- CRM needs.
- Google Calendar access.
- Google Sheets or CRM access.
- Reporting needs.
- Internal notification channel.

## WhatsApp

- Meta Business Portfolio status.
- WhatsApp Business Account status.
- Dedicated number status.
- Required message templates.
- Opt-out wording.

## Output

After onboarding, create a client folder from `clients/_template/` and fill the approved business configuration, prompt extension, services, FAQs, booking rules, and handoff rules.
